Our Services

We offer a full range of beauty services for men and women.

Manicure

Shaping, buffing, cuticle trimming, massage, and more. We also offer dip powder.

Pedicure

A refreshing foot soak, shaping, trimming, sugar scrub, paraffin, mushroom rock massage, wrap with warm towel and more.

Cut

Women's and men's traditional and contemporary haircuts.

Color

Full service color treatments, corrections and highlights.

Private Event

Have a special day to celebrate? Book a private event with us.

Gift Certificates

Lash Extensions & Fills

For those with short or sparse lashes to increase the appearance of dense lashes.

Hair Removal

Facial

Our facials are customized to your skin type, needs, and skin care goals.
